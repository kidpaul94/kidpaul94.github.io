Hi! I'm an enthusiastic robotics and AI researcher pursuing a Ph.D. at Purdue University in the Jun Laboratory. Specializing in robotic autonomy for smart manufacturing, my academic and professional journey is driven by a passion for practical applications of robotics in solving real problems, including efficient and safe robotic algorithms and industrial AI for smart manufacturing.


Bio

Hojun is a Ph.D. student in the School of Mechanical Engineering at Purdue University, where he is advised by Prof. Martin Jun. Before coming to Purdue, he earned his B.S. in Mechanical Engineering from Purdue University, West Lafayette and M.S. in Robotics from University of Michigan, Ann Arbor. Hojun is a recipient of Winkelman Fellowship and Italian Technology Award 2025.
